# Developing data products 

###  Farenheit to Celsius conversion

It was developed an interactive app to convert farenheit to celsius. 

#### How to use the app...

It appears on your left two boxes and an slider. You have to insert a numeric value in the boxes and press the buttom convert. You will see the conversion instead of NA values in the output panel. Aditionally, you can slide for a celsius vale ang get the conversion to farenheit after pressing convert buttom.
